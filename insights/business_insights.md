# Business Insights

## Customer Distribution by Country

- The USA is the company's largest customer market with 36 customers.
- Germany ranks second with 13 customers.
- France ranks third with 12 customers.
- The customer base is concentrated in a few key countries, indicating strong market presence in those regions.

## Credit Limit Analysis

- Euro+ Shopping Channel has the highest credit limit in the customer portfolio at 227,600.
- Customers with higher credit limits may represent strategically important accounts with greater purchasing capacity.
- Credit limits vary substantially across customers, suggesting different customer segments and credit policies.

## Credit Limit Statistics

- The highest credit limit is 227,600.
- The lowest credit limit is 0, indicating that some customers do not have an assigned credit facility.
- The average customer credit limit is 67,659.02.
- The difference between the maximum and average credit limit indicates an uneven distribution of credit limits across customers.

## Customer Segmentation by Credit Limit

- Most customers (60) fall within the Medium Credit category.
- 25 customers are classified as High Credit customers, representing the company's highest credit segment.
- 24 customers have no assigned credit limit, accounting for nearly 20% of the customer base.
- Only 13 customers fall into the Low Credit category.
- The majority of customers fall into the Medium and High Credit categories, indicating that a substantial portion of the customer base has moderate to high credit capacity.
