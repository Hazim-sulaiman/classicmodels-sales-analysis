## Credit Limit Analysis

- Euro+ Shopping Channel has the highest credit limit in the customer portfolio at 227,600.
- The company appears to allocate significantly larger credit limits to selected high-value customers.
- Credit limits vary substantially across customers, indicating different customer tiers and purchasing capacities.

## Credit Limit Analysis

- The highest credit limit is 227,600.
- The lowest credit limit is 0, indicating that some customers do not have an assigned credit facility.
- The average customer credit limit is 67,659.02.
- The gap between the maximum and average credit limit suggests that a small number of customers have significantly higher purchasing power than the rest of the customer base.
